# TAM, SAM, SOM Analysis for Moonshot

## TAM (Total Addressable Market)
*All mobile apps globally that could benefit from optimization*

### To Research:
- [ ] Total number of mobile apps on App Store & Google Play
- [ ] % of apps with 10K+ MAU (Monthly Active Users)
- [ ] Average revenue per app by category
- [ ] Global mobile app revenue trends

## SAM (Serviceable Available Market)
*Indie apps with traction but under-monetized*

### Criteria:
- 15K - 1M users
- Indie/solo developers (not VC-backed)
- Current revenue < $10K/month
- Non-gaming focus (utilities, education, lifestyle)

### To Research:
- [ ] Number of apps matching our criteria
- [ ] Geographic distribution (US-focused initially?)
- [ ] Revenue potential by category

## SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
*Realistic capture in Years 1-3*

### Assumptions:
- Year 1: 5-10 apps
- Year 2: 20-30 apps
- Year 3: 50+ apps

### To Research:
- [ ] Conversion rate from outreach to partnership
- [ ] Average time to close a partnership deal
- [ ] Competitive landscape - how many apps are already partnered?
