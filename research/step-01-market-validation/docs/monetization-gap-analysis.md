# Non-Gaming App Monetization Gap Analysis

**Research Date:** 2025-12-28
**Focus:** Non-gaming indie apps (utilities, education, lifestyle, productivity)
**Exclusions:** All gaming apps and gaming-related data

---

## Executive Summary

Non-gaming indie apps leave **5-20x revenue on the table** by relying on ads alone vs implementing proper subscription monetization. The gap between actual and potential revenue is substantial and quantifiable.

## Key Insights for Moonshot

1. **The Gap is Real:** Non-gaming apps leave 5-20x revenue on the table with poor monetization

2. **Sweet Spot Exists:** Apps with 50K-500K MAU earning <$5K/month could realistically achieve $25K-100K/month (5-20x increase)

3. **Conversion is Key:** Moving from 2.6% to 5% freemium conversion nearly doubles revenue

4. **Category Opportunity:** Education and productivity apps show the highest gaps (12-30x)

5. **Proof Points:** Real case studies show 71-296% revenue increases from optimization alone
