Strategic Implications

What this means for Moonshot's business model.

TAM is Massive

We estimated "thousands" of target apps. The real number is ~347,000. Even capturing 0.1% means hundreds of partnerships.

99firms: 4.19M total apps

10x Revenue is Achievable

Moving an app from bottom quartile ($5-20/mo) to median ($50) is only 2.5x. Moving to top 25% validates our 10x hypothesis.

RevenueCat quartile data

Abandoned Apps = Opportunity

1M+ abandoned apps still have active users generating $53M/quarter in ad revenue. Developers may welcome resurrection partners.

Pixalate Q3 2024 Report

Geographic Arbitrage

North American apps monetize 4x better than global average. Helping non-US developers reach NA monetization levels is valuable.

RevenueCat geographic data
