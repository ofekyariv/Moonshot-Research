FAQ Content

Addressing common developer concerns based on persona research.

Ownership & Control

Will I lose ownership of my app?

No. You retain 100% IP ownership. We never take equity or ownership rights. Our partnership is purely operational—we help you monetize better, you stay the owner. This is non-negotiable.

Can I exit the partnership if it's not working?

Yes. We include a 30-day exit clause in every partnership agreement. If you're not happy with the results or the relationship, you can walk away with no penalty. No lock-ins, no strings attached.

What decisions do I still control?

You control product direction, features, and user experience. We focus on monetization strategy, pricing optimization, and growth. Major changes always require your approval.

Revenue & Economics

How does the revenue share work?

We use a 50/50 split on incremental revenue—meaning we only share the revenue we help generate above your current baseline. If we don't grow your revenue, we don't earn anything.

Are there any upfront costs?

No. We don't charge setup fees or require upfront investment. Our incentives are aligned with yours—we only make money when you make more money.

What if my revenue doesn't increase?

If we don't increase your revenue within 90 days, you can exit with no penalty. We take on the risk, not you. Our track record shows 3-10x improvements for qualifying apps.

Process & Time

How much of my time will this take?

2 hours per month maximum. We handle implementation, optimization, and operations. You're only involved for approvals and monthly check-ins. Perfect for developers with full-time jobs.

What will you actually do to my app?

We focus on monetization optimization: paywall testing, pricing strategy, ad placement, subscription funnel improvements. We can also help with ASO, analytics, and user retention—but never touch core features without your approval.

Trust & Legitimacy

How do I know you're legitimate?

We're a registered business with verifiable founders. You can check our LinkedIn profiles, review our partnership agreements with legal counsel, and speak with current partners before committing.

Will you share my data or user information?

Never. Your users, your data. We sign NDAs, implement strict data handling protocols, and never have access to user credentials or PII. Privacy is paramount.

Trust Signals Strategy

Building credibility as an early-stage partnership program.

Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Use Now)

Founder Credibility

Display founder LinkedIn profiles, past experience, and domain expertise. "Built by former [Company] growth lead with 10+ years in mobile monetization."

Problem Validation via Data

Cite market research prominently: "83% of indie apps make less than $1K/month" (RevenueCat). Shows deep problem understanding.

Transparent Process

Visual flowchart showing partnership stages. Sample revenue projections with clear assumptions. Full partnership agreement available on request.

Risk Reversal Guarantees

"No upfront costs. 30-day exit clause. 100% IP ownership retained. We only win when you win."

Pilot Program Positioning

"Join our founding cohort of 10 developers." Creates exclusivity and explains early-stage status honestly.

Phase 2-3: Post-Launch (Build Over Time)

Case Studies with Metrics

"Increased [App Name]'s MRR from $500 to $2,100 in 4 months." Include app category, developer photo, and specific strategies used.

Video Testimonials

79% more effective than text. Feature 1-2 minute clips of partners discussing results and experience.

Partner Logo Strip

"Trusted by 20+ indie developers" with app icons. Only add after having real partners.

Interactive Revenue Calculator

Input current MRR and users, see projected revenue. Increases engagement by 58% and conversion by 40%.

Press & Thought Leadership

"As featured in..." badges. Blog posts, Indie Hackers posts, Twitter presence.

Priority Improvements

1

Add Trust Signals Above the Fold

Include risk reversal messaging immediately visible: "100% IP ownership retained. No upfront costs. 30-day exit clause." Currently missing from hero section.

2

Update CTA Button Text

Change "Partner With Us" to "See If You Qualify" or "Apply for Partnership". Lower commitment language increases conversions by 26%.

3

Add Market Validation Data

Include prominent stat: "83% of indie apps make less than $1K/month" with source. Shows problem understanding and validates target market.

4

Replace Stats Section

Current "10x Average Revenue Increase" needs proof. Before having case studies, replace with trust signals: "100% IP retained", "Zero upfront cost", "30-day exit clause".

5

Add FAQ Section

Include 5-7 questions addressing key objections (ownership, time commitment, exit options). Place before final CTA. Use accordion style for mobile-friendliness.

6

Improve Mobile CTA Placement

Add sticky CTA button at bottom of mobile view. Current design may push CTA below fold on smaller devices. 60%+ of traffic is mobile.

7

Add Founder/Team Section

Display founder LinkedIn, past experience, and domain expertise. Critical trust signal for early-stage: "Built by former [Company] growth lead."

8

Simplify "How It Works"

Current 3-step process is good. Add visual flowchart/diagram showing partnership timeline with specific milestones: "Week 1-2: Audit | Week 3-8: Implementation | Month 3+: Optimization".

9

Add Revenue Model Visual

Create before/after comparison or simple calculator showing: "Your current: $500/mo -> With Moonshot: $2,000/mo (your take: $1,250)". Makes value concrete.

10

Position as "Founding Cohort"

Add scarcity/exclusivity messaging: "Accepting 10 developers for our founding cohort." Explains early-stage honestly while creating urgency.
