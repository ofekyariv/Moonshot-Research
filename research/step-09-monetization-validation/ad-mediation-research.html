Executive Summary

â—† Key Insights

  • Platform Recommendation: AdMob is best for indie developers (15K-1M MAU) due to zero fees, ease of use, and excellent fill rates (90%+)
  • eCPM Performance: Interstitial ads outperform banners by 4,094% on average. US interstitial eCPMs peak at $8.57, with rewarded video delivering 3.5x higher retention

Moonshot's Default Ad Stack Recommendation

  • Poor ad placement can destroy an app's value proposition and retention

AdMob Mediation Best Practices

  1. Enable Auto-Optimization: Let AdMob's algorithm prioritize highest-paying networks
  2. Set eCPM Floor: Minimum thresholds ensure fewer, higher-quality ads
  3. Use Frequency Capping: Limit ad exposure per user per time period
  4. Segment User Groups: Create separate ad units for new users vs power users
  5. A/B Test Placements: Test different timing/frequency combinations
  6. Monitor Key Metrics: eCPM, fill rate (85%+), retention rates, ARPDAU

What NOT to Do

  • Show interstitial during critical user tasks (mid-form, during active editing)
  • Place banners near action buttons (accidental clicks destroy trust)
  • Show ads on first app open (let users understand value first)
  • Auto-play video ads with sound (massive UX violation)
  • Ignore Google's ad policy (account bans are permanent)

Special Considerations

Critical Alert: New COPPA Rules (January 2025)

The FTC finalized major changes to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act in January 2025, significantly limiting companies' ability to monetize children's data.

â—† Restrictions for Apps Targeting Children Under 13

  • Behavioral Advertising Banned: Cannot use personal data for targeted advertising
  • Contextual Ads Only: Ads must be based on content context, not user tracking
  • No Persistent Identifiers: Severely limits ad network tracking capabilities
  • Parental Consent Required: For any data collection beyond basic functionality

Moonshot Strategy for Education Apps

Given COPPA restrictions, education apps targeting children should:

  1. Prioritize Subscription Monetization: Ads are too limited/risky
  2. Family Plans: Market to parents, not kids
  3. School/District Licensing: B2B model avoids individual data collection
  4. If Using Ads: Use COPPA-compliant networks only (eCPMs will be very low)
  5. Age Gating: Apps for 13+ can use standard ad monetization

Final Recommendation

Moonshot's Default Ad Stack

Platform: Google AdMob with mediation enabled

Mediated Networks:

  • Meta Audience Network (when fill rate is stable)
  • AppLovin (as mediated partner, not MAX)
  • InMobi (for Asia-focused apps)

Ad Format Priority:

  1. Interstitial ads - Highest revenue, use at natural transitions
  2. Rewarded video ads - Best UX/revenue balance, user-initiated
  3. Native ads - For content-heavy apps only
  4. Banner ads - Minimize or avoid entirely

Hybrid Monetization:

  • Implement "Remove Ads" IAP at $1.99
  • For apps with premium features: Bundle subscription ($4.99-$7.99/month)
  • Target 3-5% conversion from free to paid

Implementation Timeline:

  1. Week 1: Integrate AdMob SDK, implement interstitial + rewarded video
  2. Week 2: Add mediation partners, test fill rates
  3. Week 3: Implement "Remove Ads" IAP
  4. Week 4+: Optimize based on data, A/B test placements
