Moonshot's 10x Playbook

The path to 10x monetization through multi-vector optimization.

Playbook

1. Model Shift (3-8x impact)

Move from ad-only to hybrid (ads + subscription). This single change drives the largest improvement. Implement freemium with premium tier.

2. Conversion Optimization (1.5-3x)

Optimize onboarding, trial flows, and paywalls. Use 7-day opt-out trials. A/B test paywall timing (day 3-7). Target 5%+ free-to-paid conversion.

3. Pricing Strategy (1.3-2x)

Implement $9.99/month with $29.99/year (40% discount). Add tiered pricing. Use localized pricing for emerging markets.

4. Ad Optimization (2-3x)

Implement mediation (AdMob + AppLovin MAX). Add rewarded video for free users. Optimize placement timing. Target 85%+ fill rate.
