## Best Practices

### Personalization is Critical
- **Generic templates will be ignored**
- Reference specific features, reviews, or public posts
- Show you've actually used or researched their app
- Mention specific metrics they've shared

### Keep It Short
- Initial outreach: < 100 words
- First DM: 2-3 sentences max
- Respect their time

### Lead with Value
- Don't pitch immediately
- Offer insights or ask genuine questions first
- Position yourself as helpful, not salesy

### Timing Matters
- DM within 24-48 hours of their post/tweet
- Engage before DMing
- Don't message on weekends (lower response rates)

### Avoid Common Mistakes
- Don't mention specific revenue numbers until qualification call
- Don't copy-paste identical messages
- Don't DM without any prior engagement (on Reddit/Discord)
- Don't be pushy - two follow-ups max, then stop

## A/B Testing Suggestions

Test these variables:
- **Subject lines:** Direct ("Partnership for [App Name]") vs Curious ("Quick question about [App Name]")
- **Opening:** Reference their work vs Lead with your value prop
- **CTA:** Specific ("15-min call Tuesday?") vs Open ("Would you be open to chatting?")
- **Length:** Ultra-short (3 sentences) vs Detailed (paragraph + bullets)
- **Framing:** "Revenue share partnership" vs "Help with monetization" vs "Scaling partnership"

Track response rates by channel and template to optimize over time.
