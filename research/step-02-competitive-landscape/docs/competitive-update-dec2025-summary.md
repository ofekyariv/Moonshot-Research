## Executive Summary

### Market Validation: STRONG
- **Non-gaming apps surpassed mobile games in revenue for the first time in Q2 2025**
  - Non-gaming: $21.1B (+24% YoY)
  - Gaming: $20B (0% growth)
  - This represents a historic shift in the app economy

### Competitive Threat: HIGH but MANAGEABLE
- **Tilting Point** has officially entered non-gaming with $30M deployed (December 2025)
- However, they only provide UA funding - no operational partnership

### Key Finding: Clear Differentiation
- No competitor offers full-stack operational partnership model
- All focus on either: UA funding only, acquisition, or gaming-only publishing
- **Moonshot's combination remains unique:** Tech rewrite + Monetization + Ops + Revenue share (no acquisition)

## 1. CRITICAL THREAT: Tilting Point

### What They Did
- **March 2025:** Announced $150M UA fund (gaming + non-gaming)
- **December 2025:** First non-gaming deals deployed
  - $12M to Clean Manager (utility app by Quiet)
  - $18M to undisclosed lifestyle app
  - Total: $30M/year in non-gaming

### Their Model
- **Non-dilutive financing:** Developers retain ownership
- **Revenue-tied repayment:** Repay from UA campaign revenue
- **What they DON'T provide:**
  - ‚ùå No technical overhaul or code rewrites
  - ‚ùå No operational support (legal, accounting, infrastructure)
  - ‚ùå No revenue optimization beyond UA spend
  - ‚ùå No analytics implementation or monetization strategy

### Competitive Analysis
- **Threat Level:** HIGH - First mover with $150M capital
- **Differentiation:** Moonshot offers full operational partnership vs capital-only
- **Opportunity:** Their model creates demand for our services - developers who get funding but lack capacity to deploy it effectively

- Risk: Their $150M creates awareness, may dominate developer mindshare
- Impact: HIGH
- Mitigation: Position as "operational partner" vs "UA funder"

**Threat 2: UA Funding Commoditization**
- Risk: Multiple UA funders emerge, price competition
- Impact: MEDIUM
- Mitigation: Moonshot's value is execution, not capital

### Emerging Opportunities

**Opportunity 1: Partner with UA Funders**
- Developers getting Tilting Point/PvX funding need tech + ops support
- Strategic Play: "You got the money - we'll build the engine"
- Turns Tilting Point from competitor to lead generation

- Non-gaming > gaming + Tilting Point pivot = validation
- Use for fundraising narrative
