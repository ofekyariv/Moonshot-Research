# Hypotheses to Validate

## Core Business Hypotheses

### H1: Under-monetized indie apps exist at scale
- **Assumption:** There are 1000s of apps with 15K-1M users earning < $1K/mo
- **Validation:** App store research, community surveys
- **Finding:** 82.8% of apps earn <$1K/month (RevenueCat, 30K apps)

### H2: Developers want partners, not acquirers
- **Assumption:** Many devs prefer passive income over selling
- **Validation:** Online research of developer forums, case studies, industry reports
- **Finding:** ~75% prefer partnership; nearly all post-sale stories express regret (loss of identity, autonomy, long-term upside)

### H3: Revenue share is acceptable
- **Assumption:** Developers will accept revenue share for hands-off income (70/30 baseline, 50/50 ceiling)
- **Validation:** Competitive analysis, industry benchmarks, published revenue share models
- **Finding:** 50/50 is the absolute ceiling, not baseline. Industry standard is 70/30 (dev keeps 70%). Recommend tiered structure: 70/30 pilot -> 65/35 growth -> 60/40 scale

### H4: We can 10x monetization
- **Assumption:** Native rewrite + monetization can 10x revenue
- **Validation:** First 1-2 app partnerships
- **Finding:** 14 case studies show 1.7x-10x improvements; 10x achievable via multi-vector optimization over 6-12 months

### H5: 8-week timeline is realistic
- **Assumption:** Typical app can be rewritten in 8 weeks
- **Validation:** Technical assessment of target apps
- **Finding:** Timeline depends on complexity tier (8wk simple / 12wk medium / 16+ complex)
