Remaining Risks & Mitigations

Key risks identified through research and corresponding mitigation strategies.

Developer Acquisition MEDIUM
Convincing first developers to partner with an unproven entity. Cold outreach may have low conversion.
Mitigation: Offer 70/30 rev share (dev keeps 70%) for pilots. Build credibility through content marketing before outreach. Target "Burned Out Solo Dev" persona who has most pain.
Monetization Underperformance MEDIUM
First apps may not achieve 3-5x uplift, breaking unit economics.
Mitigation: Strict app selection criteria (100K+ MAU, clear monetization path). Conservative 3x projections, not 10x. Kill underperformers quickly (3-month checkpoint).
Technical Complexity LOW
Some apps may have hidden technical debt or complexity.
Mitigation: 8-point complexity scoring system. Technical due diligence before partnership. Start with Tier 1 (simple) apps only. Buffer 30% on timeline estimates.
Competition Entry LOW
Gaming publishers (Tilting Point, Kwalee) could expand to non-gaming.
Mitigation: First-mover advantage in non-gaming. Build relationships and case studies quickly. Gaming publishers historically avoid non-gaming due to different economics.
Developer Relationship MEDIUM
Developers may become difficult to work with or dispute revenue share.
Mitigation: Clear legal contracts (Standard LLC JV). Revenue transparency via RevenueCat dashboards. Regular check-ins and relationship management. Exit clauses for both parties.
Capital Runway LOW
$150K may not be sufficient if first partnerships take longer than expected.
Mitigation: Conservative projections show 2-3 apps feasible. Maintain $15K buffer. Lighter Capital RBF available at $15K MRR if needed. Can extend runway with consulting.
Biggest Risk: First Partnership Success
The first partnership is the highest-risk moment. Everything depends on finding the right first app and executing well. Mitigation: Be extremely selective on App #1 - prioritize "easy win" over scale. Target Tier 1 complexity with clear monetization path.
